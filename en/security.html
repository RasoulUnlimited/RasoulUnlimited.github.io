Reporting Issues

If you discover a security issue, please avoid public disclosure and use one of the following channels:

Email: rasoul.unlimited@gmail.com

GitHub: Open a security issue on GitHub

To help us triage effectively, please include whenever possible:

  • Brief description and precise location of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce or a proof-of-concept (PoC)
  • Contact information for follow-up
  • Estimated severity/impact
  • Relevant environment or configuration details
  • Links, logs or screenshots if helpful

Please allow reasonable time for investigation and mitigation before public disclosure.

Scope

Authorized security testing is strictly limited to the following domains:

  • rasoulunlimited.ir
  • www.rasoulunlimited.ir

Any testing against third-party services or infrastructure (e.g., hosting providers, payment gateways) is out of scope and must follow the respective provider’s own policies and terms.

Responsible Disclosure Process

  • In most cases, reports are acknowledged within 48 hours.
  • Depending on severity, fixes or mitigation plans are usually communicated within 30 days.
  • If you wish, your name or handle can be added to the security acknowledgments section.
  • The canonical, machine-readable policy is published at security.txt.

Testing Guidelines

Only perform non-destructive testing and avoid impacting real user data or service availability.

Do not attempt denial-of-service (DoS / DDoS) attacks, excessive traffic generation, or any testing that could degrade the service.

You are responsible for ensuring all activities comply with applicable laws and regulations. By submitting a report, you confirm that your testing was conducted lawfully.
