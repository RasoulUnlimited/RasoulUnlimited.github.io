About Me

Mohammad Rasoul Sohrabi, born on September 6, 2005, and known as "Unlimited," is a unique blend of championship athlete (official 1st Dan Karate Federation), technical developer, and scientific visionary. He is a Biomedical Engineering student at the University of Tehran (Central Campus) and a member of the university's Biomedical Engineering Scientific Association. He believes that in today's noisy world, only the authentic voice is heard. With an inherent drive to make an impact and an "unlimited" mindset, from launching the first Discord Persian bot with official Discord verification at the age of 14, to publishing articles on Virgool, and participating in university projects, he has aimed to leave his unique mark on everything he does. He seeks to creatively combine biomedical engineering and programming to bring about a revolution in digital health, taking steps beyond his age.
