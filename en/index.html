About Me

Mohammad Rasoul Sohrabi, born on September 6, 2005, and known as "Unlimited," exemplifies a rare fusion of competitive sports (official 1st Dan from the Karate Federation of the Islamic Republic of Iran), technical development, and scientific foresight. He is a Biomedical Engineering student at Islamic Azad University, Central Tehran Branch and an active member of the University's Biomedical Engineering Scientific Association, demonstrating his commitment to both academic and athletic pursuits.

Driven by an intrinsic motivation to make an impact and an "unlimited" mindset, I’ve aimed to leave my unique mark on everything I do. This ranges from launching the first Persian-language Discord bot with official Discord verification at the age of 14 ( in 2020), to publishing validating articles on Virgool and Medium, and participating in university projects with DOI identifiers, always striving for high standards.

He believes that in today's noisy world, only the genuine and authentic voice is heard. His primary goal is to creatively combine biomedical engineering and programming to revolutionize digital health, consistently stepping beyond his years in pursuit of innovation and complex problem-solving.
