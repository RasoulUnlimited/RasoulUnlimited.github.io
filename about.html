About Rasoul Unlimited

Mohammad Rasoul Sohrabi, known professionally as Rasoul Unlimited, is a biomedical engineering student at the University of Tehran and a full‑stack developer. He is best known for creating the first verified Persian Discord bot and leading a range of open‑source projects focused on digital health and personal branding. His interdisciplinary approach combines engineering, software development and content strategy, all underscored by a dedication to clean, user‑centered design. Beyond coding, he is a first‑dan karate practitioner who values discipline and continuous learning.
